Meet the Author

Bukonla Adebakin

Author

Bukonla is a seasoned professional with over ten years of experience at the intersection of media, technology, and development. She blends expertise in social impact initiatives with financial and strategic management, helping organizations expand reach, maximize impact, and strengthen stakeholder relationships. She is currently the Chief Operating Officer of Intense Group, a growth marketing agency operating in the UK and Nigeria, where she drives strategy, revenue growth, and profitability. Prior to this, she was Group COO at RED for Africa, and led/worked on The Future Project (organizers of The Future Awards Africa) for eight years. Bukonla holds an MBA from Warwick Business School, UK, and an Executive Certificate in Social Sector Management from Pan-Atlantic University, Lagos.
